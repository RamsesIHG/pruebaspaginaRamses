Who We Are

Since 1993, we have been offering in Costa Rica the best brands with the latest global trends in lighting technologies, which help improve the environment through energy savings and the user's quality of life.

Arte en Luz is a pioneering company in providing the best in innovation and technological development to Engineers, Architects, Consultants, Designers, Developers, Construction Companies, and Electromechanical firms, ensuring their lighting projects are a success.


Comprehensive Service

Arte en Luz is a national leader in automation and lighting systems, thanks to an excellent team of professionals dedicated to providing consulting, plan design, importation, training, and after-sales service.

Arte en Luz represents and distributes recognized brands and is a direct importer from factories.
